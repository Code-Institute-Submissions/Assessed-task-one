What is the international

The dota 2 international is a global E-sports compition to determin the best team of the year. The main few matches ranging across 10 days typically in late august and consists of 18 teams. Each team will be rewarded with a cash prize even if they come lest place, simply getting into the tornment alone shows enough for a share of the fortune. Hoested by valve (the owners of steam and many other ground breaking games, for example cs.go) so the money isnt short, this allows the event to be displayed incredibly in exotic locations with lare seatign areas for the public, incredible faciilitys and to make it genrally apealing and profesional for both the public and the players. This most recent international is the 8th so far which explains how it can be called ti8 (the international 8).

How long has dota been around

Dota itself actually originated from a mod on World of warcraft (a mod stands for a modification, developing your own game inside of another effictivly). This was back in 2003 when modding on world of warcraft was huge, dota (defence of the ancient) kicked off so well that valve recognised this and even though it was originally on a blizzard game created it for themselves which released in 2013, blizzard tried to contest this as they assumed they would have rights as the game was bassed of of their assests but they were incorrect. Many companies have been making MOBA's (multiplayer online battle arean) since.
