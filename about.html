What is the international?
The dota 2 international is a global E-sports compition to determin the best team of the year. It takes place over 5 days typically in late august and consists of 18 teams. Each team will be rewarded with a cash prize even if they come lest place, simply getting into the tornment alone shows enough for a share of the fortune. Hoested by valve (the owners of steam and many other ground breaking games, for example cs.go) so the money isnt short, this allows the event to be displayed incredibly in exotic locations with lare seatign areas for the public, incredible faciilitys and to make it genrally apealing.
