Grand final: OG vs PSG.LGD

The grand final of ti8, this was a best of five fight and all five fights were played.
Game 1- (OG)
The first game LGD were holding the early game, getting first blood and their carry getting picks so OG were playing defensive but as soon as LGD would get too confident OG would flip it around and counter it with aggresion allowing them to get a few good picks on ganks however while OG lost the lanes they still managed to get a fair trade in team fights. It all flipped around when the shut down spectre got an ultra kill for OG at a rosh fight allowing them to secure rosh and push upperground which LGD could not defend as team fights were not going there way, after upperground got pushed LGD could not defend it and called GG at 34 mins.
Game 2- (PSG.LGD)
The second game took a diffrent turn as it was compleatly dominated LGD, they get first blood and from the point the game goes in their way. They eliminate OG's courier early on which greatly impacted OG as they could'nt get any items. Enchantress had the top net worth in the game at 11 mins which lead to a good start allowing them to control the map and secure rosh at 26 mins and also managed to turn a losing fight allowing them to hold a huge mental advantage over OG and have a 5k gold advantage. At this point the game was so in LGD's favour as their two supports kill OG's gyro carry. At this point LDG push racks and OG called GG at 38 mins.
Game 3- (PSG.LGD)
The third game of the five intense matches was extremely agressive, OG perform a perfect play with lina sniping the courier mid before the game was to start putting LGD at an instant disadvantage however after getting the first two kills of the game top and bot the gold was instantly in their favour and even though they had no courier and couldnt get their items they still managed to win the early game playign very agressivly however the first big fight was still won by OG despite this Fy (LGD's captin) clutched a great play as him as pheonix was fighting a 3v1 and managed to kill two of them before dying meaning he could get to a higher level and making his supernova even harder to kill. By 20 mins there had been a total of 42 kills meaning with all these kills that the game could swing any way but as OG's supports have a total of 26 deaths its in the favour of LGD. After LGD feeding off of OG's supports they manage to push upperground causing OG to call gg at 34 mins.
Game 4- (OG)
The longest game out of the 4 where OG secure first blood but PSG instantly trade it and get an aditional kill on top of it. LGD ganking mid but OG's mid managing to kill 2 while dying but after that OG's mid (invoker) seems to get shut down fairly heavily but after a while of this invoker was able to come back. OG were doing a fantasic job of shutting down LGD's cores and carries which allows them to continue to play agressive which is exactly what they did and giving them map control and this allowed them to push uppground at the early of time of 26 mins. Invoker was managing to rat dota bot but only does some tier three damage. At this point LGD manage to get a few solid picks and push heavily forcing buy backs from OG as they begin to lose axe a lot but Phanton lancer and I.O do an incredible job defending and winning the fight and pushing which kept repeating untill rosh was taken by OG giving them the advantage they needed to push and force the GG from LGD.
Game 5- (OG)
The final match of the internaition, so already the preasure is on and the hype is real for the players and the spectators, from the people at home to the ones in the stadium but however was one of the more simple games, it started with LGD gettign first blood and * as earth shaker getting ganks all over the map putting LGD's network migh above OG's, with this happening for a while by the 18 min mark OG were losing 18-4 on kills as they were also losing team fights. It all changed around rosh... In one big team fight where it diddnt look promising for OG but after a few buy backs on their side this long fight came to an end in OG's favour allowing them to take rosh. This was the confidence boost that OG needed as from this point the game was theirs, they stopped loosing team fights and instead began to dominate and snowball and an incredible rate, so much so they went from having the lower team net worth to a net worth advantage of 21k in under 8 mins. One big last team fight settled it all as LGD could not keep up with this pace, when wiped it it allowed OG to push for the win and become the international 2018 champions!
